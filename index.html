    Notebooks

    Organize, and keep all your work in a simple yet efficient way with our Notebook “tree-data” like structure. Create a Notebook for each course, and store all relevant data and files with the ability to create sub-notebooks. Create notes, documents, and combine your workflow with our built-in widgets and integrations to optimize your efficiency.

    Calendar

    Keep track of all upcoming assignments, due dates and events. Create reminders, and never miss a deadline or payment ever again. Set automatic actions to occur at specific times with our integrative calendar. Create multiple calendars, and share them with others.

    Collaborate

    Collaborate with others to work on assignments simultaneously, create and update calendars for the semester, share assignments instantaneously, send messages and video call for optimal productivity, all from the comfort of one application.

    Extensions

    Take advantage of our built-in extensions and integrations for the optimal experience. Log in to your Spotify or Apple Music account to control what you listen to within your assignment window. Send emails, check the latest news, and track your stock portfolio without interrupting your workflow.
